# **App Name**: GoGreen Energy Advisory

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Content Pages: Display static and dynamic content for the Homepage, Services, How It Works, About Us, and Testimonials sections.
- Energy Audit & Consultation Request Form: A user-friendly form allowing businesses to request an energy audit or a free consultation, collecting necessary initial details.
- Case Studies & Results Visualization: Showcase successful client projects with visually compelling charts and before-and-after comparisons to demonstrate energy reduction potential.
- Interactive Energy Savings Calculator: An interactive tool that allows users to input basic information and receive an estimated calculation of potential energy and cost savings.
- AI-powered Preliminary Energy Opportunity Report: A generative AI tool that takes user inputs about their building type and energy usage to provide a preliminary, personalized report detailing potential energy savings and recommending relevant services.
- Contact & WhatsApp Integration: Dedicated contact page providing business email, location, and a direct WhatsApp messaging button for easy communication.
- Sitemap and Navigation: Clear and intuitive navigation bar and sitemap ensuring easy access to all main sections of the website.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color Scheme: A professional and clean light color scheme, with ample use of white space to enhance the minimalist feel.
- Primary Color: A deep, professional green (#307A4D) representing sustainability and corporate stability.
- Background Color: A very subtle, desaturated light green (#EEF5F1) that is nearly white, providing a fresh and clean canvas.
- Accent Color: A vibrant yet sophisticated green (#66CC66) used for calls-to-action, highlights, and interactive elements, emphasizing growth and energy.
- Neutral Text Color: A dark charcoal gray (#333333) for primary text, ensuring high readability and a professional tone against the light background.
- Headline Font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), for a modern, techy, and professional appearance in headings.
- Body Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), providing a clean, objective, and highly readable typeface for all body text and descriptions.
- Use professional, minimalist line-art icons that complement the engineering and technology aesthetic.
- Integrate engineering-style diagrams and data visualizations to clearly explain complex processes and showcase results.
- Implement a minimalist, grid-based layout with generous white space to convey sophistication and focus, akin to high-end consulting firms.
- Incorporate subtle and smooth animations for transitions and interactive elements to enhance user experience without being distracting, reinforcing a polished and modern feel.
